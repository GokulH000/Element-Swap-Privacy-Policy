Privacy Policy for Element Swap
Last Updated: April 08, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Element Swap. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data of our players. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game.

2. Information We Collect
Element Swap is a "tap to play" hypercasual game. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal details like names or email addresses. However, we may collect:

Technical Data: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID).

Gameplay Data: High scores, levels completed, and in-game preferences.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services
To keep Element Swap free to play, we display advertisements. These third-party ad partners may collect and use data to show relevant ads.

Advertising IDs: Our partners may use your device's advertising ID to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

Third-Party SDKs: Our game may include tools from partners (such as Unity Ads or AdMob). We encourage you to check their respective privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy
Element Swap is intended for a general audience. Because the game is accessible to children:

We do not knowingly collect "Personal Information" (as defined by COPPA) from children under the age of 13 without complying with legal requirements.

In-game ads are served through providers that support age-appropriate filtering.

5. Data Retention and Security
We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Any technical data used by third-party services is managed according to their security protocols.

6. Your Rights
Depending on your location (such as the EU under GDPR), you may have the right to:

Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Request the deletion of data collected by our third-party partners.
